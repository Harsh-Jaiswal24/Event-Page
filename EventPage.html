Offline Programming Quiz

Department of Computer Science & Engineering at ITS Engineering College, Greater Noida is organizing Programming Quiz on January 29, 2024 . This event is not merely a quiz competition; it is a celebration of knowledge, a testament to the intellectual prowess within our academic community. It is designed as a platform for interdisciplinary discourse, emphasizing the exchange of ideas, collaborative research, and fostering a culture of intellectual synergy within our academic community. It is a celebration of intellectual curiosity, culminating in a spirited Quiz extravaganza that promises to showcase the depth and diversity of our collective knowledge.

Date:: 26 Jan 2024
